Required inputs

What a seller needs to enter when applying

The draft application combines business identity, circular operations data, funding requests, compliance evidence, and storeowner material requests in one package.

Store identity and contact details

Confirm who is applying, where the store operates, and how reviewers can reach the lead contact.

Operations and sustainability profile

Show how the business keeps clothing in use and what share of inventory already aligns with UNCLS goals.

Funding request and use of funds

Specify how much support is requested and which parts of the resale operation the grant would strengthen.

Supporting documents and evidence

Reviewers need a basic operating record, proof of the business, and evidence behind the sustainability claims.

Storeowner materials and compliance review

Choose whether the store wants the shopper-facing advert package, annual certification review, or both.

This draft page is a checklist and mock application form. It does not transmit data. After preparing the information above, use the contact page to submit the package to the UNCLS team.

Prepare these documents

Before submitting, gather the core records that support the form fields and make the review faster.

  • Business registration or seller verification
  • Basic budget and use-of-funds plan
  • Inventory or sourcing summary
  • Repair, diversion, or sustainability evidence
  • Storefront photos or customer communication examples
Connected advert

Shopper benefits advert for supported stores

This is the customer-facing advertisement a supported store can give shoppers or display in-store. It explains discount and repair benefits linked to secondhand and natural-material clothing purchases.

  • Connect it to the customer education request in the form.
  • Use it in-store, online, or in local outreach materials.

Connected certificate

Annual sustainability compliance certificate

Stores that comply with UNCLS standards can be reviewed for this certificate. It functions as a public-facing proof point that the seller meets the framework's sustainability expectations.

  • Connect it to the certification review request in the form.
  • Requires yearly review and renewal for continued use.
